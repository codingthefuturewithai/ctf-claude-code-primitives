# Statement of Work (SOW)
## In‑Depth AI‑Native Strategy Session

This Statement of Work (“SOW”) is entered into pursuant to the Consulting Services Agreement between **Tim Kitchens / Coding the Future** (“Consultant”) and **Client**. This SOW defines a discrete, paid engagement focused on strategic alignment and planning only.

---

## 1. Scope of Services

The Consultant will deliver **one (1) in‑depth strategy session** focused on establishing a shared understanding and draft direction for an AI‑native transformation. Services include:

- Review of Client‑completed AI‑Guided Intake Interview (provided by Client)
- Preparation of a focused discussion plan for the session
- Facilitation of a **60‑minute live strategy session** with designated Client participants
- Strategic discussion covering:
  - Current AI usage patterns and workflow gaps
  - Pilot framing (low‑risk, non‑delivery‑critical)
  - Tooling and governance considerations
  - Success signals and constraints
- Identification of draft recommendations and next‑step options

All outputs are **non‑binding drafts and decisions‑in‑principle**, not final implementation plans.

---

## 2. Out of Scope

The following are explicitly excluded from this SOW:

- Hands‑on implementation or configuration
- Ongoing advisory or follow‑up sessions
- Stakeholder coordination outside the scheduled session
- Custom documentation beyond session notes
- Project management or delivery oversight

Any additional services require a separate SOW.

---

## 3. Deliverables

- Live 60‑minute strategy session
- High‑level verbal summary of findings and options
- Identification of recommended next steps

No written report is guaranteed unless explicitly agreed in a subsequent SOW.

---

## 4. Client Responsibilities

- Completion of the AI‑Guided Intake Interview prior to the session
- Timely provision of accurate information
- Attendance of required decision‑makers at the session
